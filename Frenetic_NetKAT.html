NetKAT Syntax

The NetKAT language is central to Frenetic, and we factor out the central types here. The big actors on NetKAT structures are Frenetic_NetKAT_Compiler which compiles NetKAT into flow tables, Frenetic_NetKAT_Parser which turns NetKAT strings (e.g "TcpSrcPort(8080); port := 2") into NetKAT, and Frenetic_NetKAT_Json which turns JSON-formatted NetKAT into NetKAT
