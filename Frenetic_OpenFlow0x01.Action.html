type sequence = Frenetic_OpenFlow0x01.action list 
val move_controller_last : sequence ->
       sequence
move_controller_last seq produces a semantically-equivalent list of actions with actions that send packets to the controller moved to the end. This works around a known bug in the OpenFlow reference switch where actions in an action sequence after a "send to controller" (Output (Controller n)) action are ignored.
